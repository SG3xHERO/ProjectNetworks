About Us

Why Join Project Networks?

Project Networks is a UK-based server network offering a place for everyone to play games together. We run seasonal servers - when a season is active, jump in and play! When a season closes, we take a break until the next one kicks off.

Currently running Minecraft Season 9 and looking to expand with Terraria and other games. It's all about having fun and playing together during active seasons. No stress, just good times with a great community.
